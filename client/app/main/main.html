Frequently Asked Questions

What is a logbook loan ?

A logbook loan is a loan product secured against your vehicle. It allows you to unlock the value in your vehicle and keep driving it as normal. We will hold on to your logbook for the duration of your loan agreement.

What will happen to my car ?

A logbook loan is secured on your vehicle. We will keep hold of your logbook (v5) for the full duration of the loan, but you keep driving the vehicle. Once the loan is repaid you will receive the v5 document back.

What documents do i need ?

Before we payout your loan we need to see your vehicle's V5 document (logbook), payslips or bank statements showing prove of income.

How will i get the money ?

Once your loan has been approved, we can transfer the funds directly in your bank account within 1 hour.

How much can i borrow ?

You can borrow £300 - £5000. The amount you are eligible to borrow will be calculated based on the value of your vehicle and how much you can afford to pay on a monthly basis.

How long are your loans for ?

We offer a flexible solution with loans ranging from 6 months to 24 months.

What vehicles do you lend against ?

We lend against vehicles of any value or age providing that they are roadworthy, taxed, MOT’d and insured. We accept cars and vans.

Will i be credit checked ?

As a responsible lender we undertake credit checks to establish the loan is affordable but we lend to people with bad credit history.

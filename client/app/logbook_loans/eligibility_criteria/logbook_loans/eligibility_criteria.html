Eligibility Criteria

Please ensure you meet our eligibility criteria before applying

You must be able to prove you can afford the loan

Once you have applied for one of our logbook loans, one of our experienced underwriters will call you in person to go through the application. They will answer any questions you may have, talk you through a quote and work out the most affordable monthly repayments over a term that is suitable. Please note, as a responsible lender we only offer loans to people we think can afford to pay them back and never guarantee approval.

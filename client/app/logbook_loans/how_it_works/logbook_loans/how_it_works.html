How it Works

How Our Logbook Loans work in 5 simple steps

Online application

Applying for a driver loan only takes 5 minutes. To process your loan request we need some details about your vehicle and also about yourself. Once you have applied online, one of our experienced underwriter will call you on the number you provided to talk you through your loan and answer any questions you may have.

Pre-payout phonecall

Our underwriter will call you at the time requested your requested for to complete your loan application. They will also take your bank account details to set up your monthly direct debit repayment and to also pay your loan into. They will also carry out an affordability check with you this is establish that you can afford to make the monthly repayment. The underwriter would also ask for some details of your vehicle's registration document so its a good idea to have it close by.

Send documents and sign agreement electronically

There is no face to face appointment, you simply send us the required documents and sign your loan agreement electronically via your smartphone. Don't worry our underwriter will talk you through the process. We are the only logbook lender that processes our loan this way which is why we are the cheapest logbook lender in the UK.


Once we receive your documents and signature, we payout your loan

After your loan is complete

As soon as your loan is complete our accounts team are notified and your V5 Logbook (and key if applicable) is sent back to you by recorded mail. This is usually received within 48 hours of the loan being repaid. At this stage the legal title of the vehicle passes back to you, leaving you free to do as you please with the car!
